Training Division

CAREER SERVICES OFFERED

LEARNING AND DEVELOPMENT

Learning & Development phase of students are facilitated at SKCT through Androgogical teaching learning process along with frequent industrial visits, industry expert interactions, Industrial Training, and Project Guidance from industrial mentors. Feedback from our recruiters and other stakeholders form a major input during curriculum and syllabi revision meetings at SKCT. This enables in the development of an industry oriented curriculum & syllabi at our campus.

PLACEMENTS

SKCT is known among its peers for consistent and top Placement Records. More than 150 recruiters visit our campus every year to absorb our students on an early hiring basis. With a well established reputation of our alumni, recruiters are happy in connecting with us every year. Training & Placement Division at SKCT ensures internships and industrial training programs and full time placement offers to students from all branches of Engineering and Management. The division also sees to the improvement in the quality of placements in terms of opportunities, recruiters and salary packages.

CORPORATE RELATIONS

SKCT has established a strong rapport with various top industries and academia round the globe. MoUs with top corporates has enabled in skilling up of our students to a larger extent and in making our graduates employable and industry ready. The companies has extended their mentoring services for our students right from the day they are tagged as SKCTians. Knowledge transfer on latest technological developments and real time projects offered are added advantages of the understandings established with these corporates. Students are also provided with opportunities for On Job Training / Internships during summer / winter breaks.

CORPORATE COMMUNICATION

At SKCT, right from the inception the students get involved in various activities for equipping them with professional skills making them industry ready. SKCT has an ecosystem helping in the overall personality development of the students. Group Discussions, Extramural lectures, Snap talks, Theater club performances and individual blogs contribute for enhancing the communication & leadership skills among our students. Interaction with Industry experts at regular intervals along with specialized certification courses on language finds ways for corporate communication among our students.

CORPORATE TRAINING

Training & Placement Division at SKCT organises specialized training programs and assists students in improving their employability skills. SKCT has tied up with industrial partners in imparting skill based training for students across various disciplines. Our alumni also plays a major role in this ecosystem of corporate level training. Students are also encouraged to take up online certification courses on trending techs, getting them equipped with changing industry requirements.

CENTRE FOR FOREIGN LANGUAGE

An exclusive centre for foreign language is active at SKCT. Regular training programs on foreign languages happen round the year and students across various branches of engineering and management get benefited out of it. These programs makes our students ready for the global market.
