CENTER OF EXCELLENCE

The Centre of Excellence for Finance (CoE) has been established in the year 2017 by SKCT-SoM for the development of faculty and students in the areas Accounting & Finance, Econometrics, Financial Markets, Financial Derivatives and Risk Management. In order to enrich the faculty and student knowledge business research software’s like SPSS, Excel, E-Views, Gretl and R software’s are available in CoE Finance. The objective of the centre is to provide industry-oriented practical training in the areas of Stock simulation, Stock Trading, Financial Modeling, Mock Trading and Placement Training related to finance. The CoE-finance supports 60 students with individual computers.

The outcome of this Centre of Excellence-Finance is to inculcate research knowledge in the areas of Stationarity Analysis, Descriptive statistics, Normality test, Time series analysis, Correlation, Cointegration, Causality, Forecasting by using Simple and Multiple Regression, ARIMA, ARCH, GARCH and financial data analytics.

The following work has been carried out with the help of the Centre of Excellence- Finance.

  • Risk-Return analysis
  • Fundamental and Technical Analysis
  • Stationarity analysis
  • Econometric Analysis
  • Determining the cost of capital
  • Capital structure decision
  • Capital budgeting decisions
  • Financial Statement Analysis

DEMING.COM

William Edwards Deming is widely acknowledged as the leading management thinker in the field of quality. He was a statistician and business consultant whose methods helped hasten Japan’s recovery after the Second World War and beyond. He derived the first philosophy and method that allowed individuals and organizations to plan and continually improve themselves, their relationships, processes, products and services. His philosophy is one of cooperation and continual improvement; it avoids blame and redefines mistakes as opportunities for improvement. And that’s how we coined our club name as Deming.com It is a platform to nurture and enhance theoretical foundations and applications in the field of Operations management. This Deming.com club of SKCT-SOM has been formed with an objective of sharing knowledge and information in the areas of Systems and Operations. Members are updated on the latest trends and developments in these fields by regular club meetings. This club help students to learn about all backgrounds on industry trends and the growing need for an analytical mindset through tool demonstrations, guest speakers, networking opportunities and more.
